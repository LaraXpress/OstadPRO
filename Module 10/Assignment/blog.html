January 15, 2023

PM orders strengthening efforts to unlock potentials of blue economy

Prime Minister Sheikh Hasina has directed the authorities concerned to take strong efforts to unlock the potential of blue economy in the vast sea area of the country. She made the directive while chairing the meeting of the Executive Committee of the National Economic Council (Ecnec) at the NEC conference room in the city's Sher-e Bangla Nagar area today. Briefing reporters after the meeting, Planning Minister MA Mannan said the premier asked the authorities concerned to take cogent efforts to unlock the potential of the blue economy.

January 31, 2023

Bangladesh requests code share between Air Canada and Biman

Bangladesh has requested Canada to increase air connectivity between the two countries through code sharing of Air Canada and other international airlines with Biman Bangladesh Airlines. Air Vice Marshal Mafidur Rahman, chairman of Civil Aviation Authority of Bangladesh, made the request while meeting with Lilly Nicholls, the high commissioner of Canada at the former's office in Kurmitola yesterday.

March 1, 2023

Banks allowed to open foreign currency accounts for local shippers

The central bank has allowed banks to open foreign currency accounts in the names of shipping companies, airlines and freight forwarders to support their global operations. In view of the growing trend of global operations by shipping companies and airlines, banks may open FC accounts in the names of the companies having global operations, said the Bangladesh Bank in a notice today.
